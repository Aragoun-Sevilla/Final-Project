Tracen Academy Summary

Nihon Kiyouin Training Center Facility Dossier

The Nihon Kiyouin Training Center (widely recognized as Tracen Academy) is a legendary, world-class educational facility built explicitly to foster the athletic development and theatrical performance styles of elite horse girls.

Our training campus bridges standard high-school academic curriculum with intense high-performance attribute tracking. Equipped with cutting-edge turf track loops, weight simulation centers, and dedicated vocal guidance mirrors, we ensure every runner is fully prepared to cross the G1 racing finish line before stealing the theater spotlights during post-race Winning Live concerts.

Our Mission

At Tracen Academy, our mission is to provide an elite educational and athletic ecosystem where horse girls can cultivate their innate gifts. By balancing premium scholastic learning with cutting-edge track regimens, we equip our competitors to capture historic G1 racing crowns while developing the artistic vocal performance skills needed to command the global entertainment stage.

Our Vision

We envision a unified community of strategic excellence where trainers and horse girls work hand-in-hand to redefine racing history. Through modern facility diagnostic frameworks, data-driven optimization, and healthy competitive tracking, we aim to remain the world's leading training grounds for developing historic Twinkle Series champions.

🏃 Speed Training (スピード)

Directly calculates your competitor's absolute top terminal velocity during straight-away segments. Maximizing this attribute is crucial for front-running pacing configurations, allowing runners to gain ground early and hold their position through crowded packs.

  • Primary Target: Top Speed Capacity
  • Secondary Gain: +Power Sub-points

🔋 Stamina Training (スタミナ)

Stamina values dictate the total engine fuel and endurance reserves available to your runner. Without adequate stamina investment, horse girls are highly vulnerable to dropping their speed tier rapidly or gasping for air long before approaching the final stretch.

  • Primary Target: Endurance Capacity & Fuel Pool
  • Secondary Gain: +Guts Sub-points

💪 Power Training (パワー)

Power controls torque, acceleration bursts, and overtaking force. High power allows a horse girl to burst out of the starting gate cleanly and weave safely through tight clusters of rival runners.

  • Primary Target: Acceleration & Overtaking Force
  • Secondary Gain: +Stamina Sub-points

❤️ Guts Training (根性)

Guts govern your runner's mental fortitude, tenacity, and energy efficiency when under pressure. High guts metrics help horse girls withstand late fatigue and maintain peak racing momentum during intense, head-to-head home stretch sprints.

  • Primary Target: Home Stretch Endurance & Fuel Efficiency
  • Secondary Gain: +Speed & Power Sub-points

🎓 Wit Training (賢さ)

Wit (Intelligence) dictates race awareness, positioning choices, and skill trigger check odds. High wit prevents horse girls from tracking into bad paths, reduces the risk of random panic anomalies, and keeps them in optimal position throughout the race.

  • Primary Target: Tactical Awareness & Skill Activation Odds
  • Secondary Gain: +Energy Recovery Actions
